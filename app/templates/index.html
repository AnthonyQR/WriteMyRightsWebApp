Have you been laid off? Write my rights will help you get the severance payment you deserve. Get a customized letter — written by us, but sent by you — to notify your ex-employer about the compensation they owe you. It’s fast and easy.

Just fill out the form below and you’ll be on your way to a professionally written letter in your inbox.


Why do I need this?

Everyday legal problems — for workers, neighbours and consumers — often aren’t “worth” enough to hire a lawyer.

But instead of paying up to $300 per hour for legal advice, you’ll get a letter for a fraction of that cost. That’s because this isn’t specific legal advice tailored to your situation. It’s a way for you to vocalize what you want — professionally, and persuasively.


How does this work?

You complete a short survey. You’ll then get an email from us, letting you know when the letter will be ready. We then “ghostwrite” a letter and email it to you, ready for you to review and send to your (ex-)employer.

Many people in this situation do nothing. We’re helping you to take the first step.


What's this cost?

Think of this as a small investment in your rights as a worker. Once you complete the form below, we’ll be in touch about pricing. You won’t be charged at this time.

Once the letter is ready, payment is processed securely through PayPal.



Who is behind these letters?

A small team of start-up lawyers. We’ve been helping our clients, friends and families with legal issues for years, and want to expand this service to the general public.
